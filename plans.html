How Pro activation works

After completing your payment via OxaPay, your account will be automatically upgraded to Pro within a few minutes. Make sure you're logged in before purchasing. If your plan is not updated within 15 minutes, contact us at support@shadon.io with your payment transaction ID.

Frequently asked questions

What payment methods are accepted? +
Pro subscriptions are processed via OxaPay, which accepts major cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, Litecoin, and more. This ensures privacy and fast processing for our users globally.
When does my Pro plan activate? +
Your account is upgraded automatically after payment confirmation, usually within 1–5 minutes. You'll see the Pro badge appear in your dashboard once activated.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Pro is billed monthly and you can cancel anytime. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period. Contact support@shadon.io to cancel.
Is there a free trial for Pro? +
We currently don't offer a free trial for Pro, but the Free plan gives you a solid taste of the platform. You can upgrade to Pro anytime from your dashboard.
What is Shadon for? +
Shadon is an OSINT aggregation platform designed for security researchers, penetration testers, journalists, and investigators. It is intended for lawful and ethical use only. Users must comply with applicable laws in their jurisdiction.
